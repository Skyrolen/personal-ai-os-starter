# Digest

Morning briefing. One command, full picture of the day.

## Instructions

1. **Pull everything at once:**
   - Today's events from Google Calendar
   - Unread emails in Gmail — flag anything urgent or action-needed
   - Open tasks from 40_Admin/Tasks.md if it exists
   - Current goals and this week's focus from 40_Admin/goals.md

2. **Build the briefing in this format:**

## Good morning, Serif. Here's your day.

### Today's schedule
[List calendar events with times — if empty, say "Your day is open"]

### Emails needing attention
[Max 3 — urgent or action-needed only. Everything else = ignore]

### Open tasks for today
[From Tasks.md today section — if empty, say "No tasks logged yet"]

### This week's focus
[One line pulled from goals.md]

### One thing to protect today
[The single most important thing not to let slip — be specific]

3. **Keep it short.** No fluff. No long explanations.
   If something needs action, say what the action is.

4. **Do not save this file.** It's a live read every morning.

## When to Use
Every morning before starting work. Takes 10 seconds to run.
Type: /digest
